Launching the 2021 Buckets Community Survey

Dec 4, 2021 • Matt

We’re pleased to announce our third annual-except-for-2020-because-well-it-was-2020 Buckets Community Survey! Whether you use Buckets or not, your feedback will help us understand Buckets' strengths and weaknesses, get to know who’s using it and help determine where we go in the future.

It shouldn’t take longer than 3-5 minutes. Submissions will be accepted for a month or two depending on the response rate. After the survey closes, we’ll publish results here on this blog.

See 2019’s survey results here.

Thank you for your time!

— Matt & Christy
